Mission and Vision

The Arts Fund Endowment exists solely for the charitable purposes of the Arts Fund of the Allied Arts Council and for insuring the long-term financial security of the Arts Fund.

The Arts Fund Endowment ensures an accessible, enriching, vibrant arts community for current and future generations.
